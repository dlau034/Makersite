02 · The signature move

720 layers.
One vase.

Scroll — the machine does the rest. This is exactly how FDM works: a 144 mm vase at 0.2 mm layer height.

Sliced. Strata Studio cuts your model into 720 horizontal contours — toolpaths, not pixels. Walls, infill and skin become roughly 40,000 G-code moves.

Extruded. PLA melts at 210 °C and is drawn through a 0.4 mm nozzle as a 1.9 mm ribbon, placed to ±0.05 mm. Pressure advance keeps corners sharp and walls even.

Fused. Each bead is laid while the last is still warm, so layers bond chemically — not just mechanically. Strength is cumulative; Z is no longer the weak axis.
